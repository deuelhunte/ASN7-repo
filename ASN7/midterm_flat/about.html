Who I Am

Music has always been at the centre of my life. From experimenting with early recordings on a cassette tape to building a makeshift studio in my bedroom, I’ve learned to appreciate both the art and the science of making music. This website is my canvas for sharing that enthusiasm with you.

I divide my time between three creative pursuits: songwriting, audio engineering and recording. Each discipline feeds into the other and helps me create richer, more authentic soundscapes.

Hobbies & Skills

When I’m not writing lyrics or tinkering with EQ knobs, you’ll find me:

  • Playing acoustic and electric guitar
  • Experimenting with analogue gear and vintage microphones
  • Listening to underground artists for inspiration
  • Learning new DAWs and mixing techniques
